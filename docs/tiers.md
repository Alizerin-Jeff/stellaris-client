# Tiers

## How tiers work

Stellaris has two main tiers: **Free** and **Pro**.

The Free tier has three sub-levels (Free, L1, L2) based on how many Stellaris tokens you hold. The more tokens you hold, the more features you unlock and the lower your trading fees.

Pro is a paid subscription that unlocks everything.

## Checking your tier

Your current tier is displayed as a badge in the dashboard header. It updates automatically based on your Stellaris token balance.

## Upgrading your free tier

To unlock higher free tier levels, acquire Stellaris tokens:

- **Free L1** (1,000,000+ tokens) — Unlocks Extract, separate buy/sell trade sizes, and reduces your trading fee to 0.10%
- **Free L2** (10,000,000+ tokens) — Unlocks Defend Floor, alternate wallets, and reduces your trading fee to 0.05%

## Pro tier

Pro costs **$1,000/month** and unlocks:

- **All features** — Every operation, alternate wallets, separate trade sizes
- **No trading fees** — 0% fee on all trades
- **3 simultaneous bots** — Run up to 3 bots at once (vs 1 on Free)
- **10x throughput** — 1 transaction every 3 seconds (vs every 30 seconds on Free)
