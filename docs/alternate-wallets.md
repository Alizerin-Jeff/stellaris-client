# Alternate Wallets

## What are alternate wallets

Alternate wallets are reserved wallet addresses from your account's HD wallet, dedicated to a specific bot. They let you separate operational funds from your main wallet pool.

## When to use them

Alternate wallets are useful when you want to:

- **Separate funds by operation** — Run Inject, Extract, or Defend Floor through a dedicated wallet while Volume uses the main wallet pool
- **Track spending independently** — Each alternate wallet has its own balance, making it easy to see how much a specific operation has used
- **Isolate risk** — Keep your main wallet pool funds separate from targeted operations

## How to use them

1. Open an operation panel (Inject, Extract, or Defend Floor)
2. Toggle **Use alternate wallet** on
3. Select a wallet from the dropdown
4. The selected wallet's individual balance is displayed in the panel

Fund the alternate wallet directly by sending SOL and tokens to its address.

## Availability

Alternate wallets are available on Free L2 and Pro tiers. Requires holding 10,000,000+ Stellaris tokens (Free L2) or a Pro subscription.
